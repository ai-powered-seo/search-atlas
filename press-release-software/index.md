## What Is Press Release Software

Press Release Software functions as an end-to-end AI system for digital PR and authority building inside the Search Atlas platform. It automates headline writing, body copy, formatting, journalist targeting, distribution, syndication, and performance tracking. The tool produces editorially structured releases optimized for both media pickup and SEO, including structured data, keyword integration, and strategic backlink placement to accelerate indexing and ranking.

It integrates directly with [link building](/search-atlas/link-building) workflows, [Atlas Brain](/search-atlas/atlas-brain) for strategy, and [OTTO SEO AI](/search-atlas/otto-seo-ai) for broader authority campaigns.

## Core Capabilities and Feature Set

Press Release Software delivers comprehensive automation across the PR workflow:

- **AI Writing and Optimization**: Generates headlines, subheads, story flow, key quotes, SEO tags, and press-ready formatting aligned for maximum discoverability and media appeal
- **Multi-Channel Distribution**: Publishes across 11 distribution channels and 150+ websites in one action, including Tier 1 high-impact outlets and Tier 2 for maximum coverage
- **Content Variations**: Creates tailored versions of the same announcement for different audiences, regions, or channels with one click
- **SEO Optimization**: Automatically incorporates keywords, metadata, and backlink opportunities to boost indexing speed and search visibility
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Monitors pickups, engagement analytics, and performance data to refine future campaigns
- **Campaign Management**: Supports republishing to additional channels without extra editorial fees and provides detailed status updates
- **Hyperdrive Credit System**: Uses credits for AI writing and distribution, included in Growth and Pro plans or available for purchase

## How Press Release Software Works in Search Atlas

The workflow starts in the Authority section of the Search Atlas dashboard. Users enter target page URL, keywords, business details, images, videos, and writing instructions. The AI generates a complete, newsroom-ready press release that users can review and edit. Next, select distribution channels and options such as one version across all channels or multiple variations.

After approval, the system distributes through newswire partners, industry blogs, and journalist networks. Real-time tracking shows status, pickups, and engagement metrics. Users can republish to additional channels easily. The entire process integrates with broader Search Atlas tools, allowing [SEO topical maps](/search-atlas/seo-topical-maps) to support PR narratives and [local SEO](/search-atlas/local-seo) for location-specific announcements.
