# Report Builder: White-Label SEO and Local SEO Reporting

**Report Builder** is Search Atlas's automated reporting tool that creates professional, branded SEO and local SEO performance reports in minutes. It pulls data from rankings, Google Business Profiles, traffic sources, citations, and content performance to generate customizable PDF or white-label reports for clients, stakeholders, or internal teams.

[Explore Report Builder](https://searchatlas.com/?fpr=vip-vmai) to automate your SEO reporting and demonstrate clear value with professional dashboards.

## What Is Report Builder

Report Builder functions as a centralized reporting engine inside the Search Atlas platform. It aggregates performance metrics from [local SEO](/search-atlas/local-seo), Google Business Profile activity, [SEO topical maps](/search-atlas/seo-topical-maps), and organic rankings, then transforms them into polished, easy-to-understand reports. Users can schedule automatic delivery and apply custom branding for client-facing deliverables.

The tool integrates seamlessly with [Atlas Brain](/search-atlas/atlas-brain) for strategic insights and [OTTO SEO AI](/search-atlas/otto-seo-ai) for including automation results directly in reports.

## Core Capabilities and Feature Set

Report Builder delivers flexible and automated reporting features:

- **Automated Data Aggregation**: Pulls rankings, map pack positions, traffic, citations, review metrics, and content performance from across the Search Atlas platform
- **White-Label Customization**: Applies your logo, colors, and messaging so reports look like they come directly from your agency or brand
- **Local SEO Reporting**: Includes Google Business Profile health, local citation consistency, heatmap visualizations, and map pack ranking trends
- **SEO Performance Dashboards**: Shows keyword rankings, topical authority progress, traffic growth, and content engagement metrics
- **Scheduled Delivery**: Automatically generates and emails reports on a weekly, monthly, or custom cadence
- **Client-Specific Views**: Creates tailored reports for different locations, services, or client accounts with relevant metrics only
- **Export Options**: Generates PDF reports or interactive links that can be shared securely

Users can [get started here](https://searchatlas.com/?fpr=vip-vmai) to build their first report and combine it with data from the [site explorer](/search-atlas/site-explorer), [site auditor](/search-atlas/site-auditor), and [on-page audit tool](/search-atlas/on-page-audit-tool) for complete performance visibility.

## How Report Builder Works in Search Atlas

Creating a report begins by selecting the data sources and time period. Report Builder pulls live metrics from connected tools including Google Business Profile Galactic, local citations, and SEO topical maps. Users choose templates, add custom commentary, and apply branding elements. Once configured, reports can be generated on demand or scheduled for automatic delivery.

The system supports multi-client workflows, allowing agencies to produce consistent, professional reports across their entire portfolio with minimal manual input. Integration with [Content Genius](/search-atlas/content-genius) also enables inclusion of content performance metrics in the same reports.

## Strategic Advantages

Report Builder saves hours of manual work while delivering professional-grade documents that clearly demonstrate ROI. Agencies use it to strengthen client relationships and reduce churn by providing transparent, visual proof of progress. In-house teams benefit from consistent internal reporting that supports budget justification and strategic decisions.

The tool turns complex SEO and local SEO data into clear stories with visuals, trends, and actionable recommendations. When paired with the full Search Atlas suite, it creates a closed loop from strategy and execution to measurement and reporting.

[Discover the platform](https://searchatlas.com/?fpr=vip-vmai) and streamline your SEO reporting with Report Builder for more professional and efficient client communication.
